BEHAVIOURAL DESIGN PATTERNS

1. Observer Pattern
"Hospital Patient Monitor"
Real-world: A patient's heart rate monitor is watched by multiple systems: the nurse station display, 
the doctor's pager, the ICU alarm system, and the family notification app. When heart rate spikes, 
ALL observers are notified automatically.

2. State Pattern
"Traffic Light System"
Real-world: A traffic light cycles through Green → Yellow → Red → Green. 
Each state knows: (1) what color to display, (2) how long to stay, (3) what the next state is. 
The light itself just delegates to the current state.

3. Strategy Pattern
"Payment Methods at Checkout"
Real-world: A shopping cart needs to accept Credit Card, PayPal, or Crypto. 
Each payment method has different validation, processing, and fee calculation. 
The cart shouldn't care which method is used — it just calls "pay."
